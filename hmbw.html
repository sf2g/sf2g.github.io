« Half Moon Bay Way

Half Moon Bay Way, while somewhat impractical for commuting, is one of the more awesome ways to get to work in the bay area, if not the world. Cruise down highway 1, go up the spectacular and legburning Tunitas Creek Road, and back down Kings Mountain Road's tight switchbacks. A gorgeous ride, highly recommended if you have time.

  • distance: ~55 miles (to Google)
  • travel time: ~4.5 hours
  • elevation gain: ~4,000 feet
  • difficulty rating (1-10): 8
  • rollout point: usually Ritual Roasters (aka "RRR")
  • inventor: Brian Wickman
