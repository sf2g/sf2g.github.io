« Bayway : Colmaway

A variant of bayway that involves a jaunt up Mission (traditionally) or Alemany (more commonly these days), a spin down Hillside and a cruise past the cemeteries in Colma before re-joining bayway a bit before the airport.

  • distance: 42.5 miles (to Google)
  • travel time: ~2 1/2 hours
  • elevation gain: ~1,000 feet
  • difficulty rating (1-10): 4
  • meetup point: ritual roasters (aka "RRR"), philz coffee (aka "PCR" or Alemany/Lyell (aka "alemanyell"))
  • inventor: brett lider
