New Riders

So you're interested in SF2G? Awesome. You should be. All it takes is a strong interest in having lots of fun. Many (if not all) SF2G riders will tell you the morning bicycle commute is the best part of their day. You get a full two to three hours to ride your bike, take in some scenery and meet new people who also love having fun and riding bicycles. A side benefit, if it's your thing, you'll be in shape.
