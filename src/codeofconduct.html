SF2G Code of Conduct

SF2G is a bike riding club, not a workplace or anything like that, but we still don't tolerate dickish behavior. If the consensus is that someone's being a jerk, that person will probably be banned from the list, and there's not a lot of bureaucracy around that. The list moderators will just do it.

Categories of dickishness include:

  • harrassment of any kind
  • trollish behavior on the list
  • repeated unsafe riding practices, like running red lights or otherwise putting fellow riders at risk
  • anything that makes other people uncomfortable, done repeatedly
This is intentionally vague. We're cool, we're nice, you be cool and all will be cool.
