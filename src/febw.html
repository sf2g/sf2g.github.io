«East Bay Skyline

East Bay Skyline, aka Far East Bay Way (FEBW) is a truly epic "commute" along the lines of Half Moon Bay Way. It's long, hilly, challenging, and beautiful, with stunning views and ideal cycling roads. It does require a BART ride and a very early wakeup, so it's not really an everyday thing, but you should try it at least once. A sunny fall or spring day is ideal, as it does get pretty hot out there.

  • distance: ~60 miles (to Google)
  • travel time: ~4.5 hours
  • elevation gain: ~4000 feet
  • difficulty rating (1-10): 9
  • rollout point: Rockridge BART
  • inventor: Brett Lider
