« Fleaway

Fleaway, so-named because much of the route uses Avenida de las Pulgas (fleas), is not often ridden but is worth a try. It pretty neatly splits the habitable peninsula and includes some legit hills if that's your bag. It also "features" some narrow roads with surly morning car traffic, so be careful of that.

  • distance: ~39 miles (to Google)
  • travel time: ~2 1/2 hours
  • elevation gain: ~1700 feet
  • difficulty rating (1-10): 5
  • rollout point: Ritual Roasters (aka "RRR") or Philz Coffee (aka "PCR")
  • inventor: Brett Lider
